[ SOME FILM TAKEN AS A HOBBY ]

A passion for analog photography. I love the process of shooting on film — from choosing the right camera and film stock to developing. There's something magical about the unpredictability of the results and the patience it requires. Each shot feels more intentional, and I enjoy the nostalgic, timeless look that film gives. For me, it’s not just about taking pictures; it’s about slowing down.
